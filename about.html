Background

Our People

Meet the Team

Our experienced advocates provide strategic legal representation across criminal law, civil litigation, matrimonial disputes, commercial litigation, consumer matters, cheque bounce cases, contract drafting, MACT claims, and corporate advisory services. Serving clients across Delhi, Ghaziabad, Noida, and the NCR region.

Adv. Abhishek Gupta

Partner | Commercial Litigation & Corporate Advisory

Adv.Abhishek Gupta advises and represents businesses, entrepreneurs, and individuals across a broad range of commercial and civil matters. His practice covers commercial litigation, contract drafting and enforcement, civil recovery, consumer disputes, and cheque bounce matters under Section 138 of the Negotiable Instruments Act, alongside corporate advisory services. With a strong grasp of business and legal strategy, Adv. Gupta delivers effective representation focused on protecting his clients’ commercial interests and resolving complex disputes efficiently.

Adv. Abhishek Banerjee

Partner | Criminal, Civil & Constitutional Litigation

Abhishek Banerjee is a litigation partner practising across District Courts, High Courts, and the Supreme Court of India. His practice spans Criminal Defense — including bail, FIR quashing, appeals, and revision petitions. While in Civil Disputes involving property, contracts and recovery. He also represents clients in specialised matters such as Cyber-crime, Data Protection Laws, Environmental Law and Intellectual Property. Known for his strategic, analytical approach, he combines rigorous legal analysis with sound judgment, delivering effective outcomes with clarity, transparency, and professional integrity.

Adv. Faizan Khan

Partner | Family, Matrimonial & Personal Laws

Adv. Faizan Khan represents clients in complex matrimonial and family disputes, covering divorce litigation, child custody and guardianship, maintenance and alimony claims, domestic violence proceedings, family settlements, and succession matters. He also advises on matters governed by Muslim Personal Law. Known for his practical and solution-oriented approach, Adv. Khan combines sound legal expertise with a deep commitment to protecting his clients’ rights and interests in sensitive and high-stakes matters.
